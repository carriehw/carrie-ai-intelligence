Andrej Karpathy joins Anthropic as it preps an IPO as early as October Andrej Karpathy 加盟 Anthropic，公司最快 10 月啟動 IPO

Summary摘要

Former Tesla AI director and OpenAI founding member Andrej Karpathy has reportedly joined Anthropic. In the same week, Anthropic was reported to be in talks with Samsung on a custom Claude chip and preparing an S-1 for an IPO as early as October 2026, after a Series H valuing it at $965B. 前 Tesla AI 總監、OpenAI 創始成員 Andrej Karpathy 據報加盟 Anthropic。同一星期，Anthropic 據報正與 Samsung 商討訂製 Claude 晶片，並準備最快於 2026 年 10 月啟動 IPO——公司在 Series H 融資後估值已達 9,650 億美元。

Why it matters為何重要

Top talent, custom compute and public-market capital are consolidating around two or three frontier labs. Whoever your martech stack depends on, its roadmap is being decided now. 頂尖人才、訂製算力與公開市場資金，正集中在兩三間 frontier labs 手上。無論你的 martech stack 依賴哪一間公司，它的未來路線圖此刻正在被決定。

Carrie's Take

Most people will read this as an AI-industry story. I read it as a procurement story: the enterprise AI market is becoming a two-horse race, and agencies that build on only one platform are taking on concentration risk their clients never signed off on. 大部分人會把這則當成 AI 行業新聞。我把它視為採購新聞：企業 AI 市場正演變成兩強競賽，只押注單一平台的 agency，其實是替客戶承擔了他們從未同意過的集中風險。

My Prediction我的預測

By mid-2027, "multi-model strategy" will appear in agency RFPs the same way "multi-cloud" appeared in IT RFPs five years ago. 到 2027 年中，「multi-model strategy」將如同五年前 IT 界的「multi-cloud」一樣，開始出現在 agency 的 RFP 之中。
